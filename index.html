Sports Academies

Wadi Degla Clubs offer a huge variety of sports through 20 different academies; all applying the latest sports curriculum and led by professional coaches. Our aim is to continue building a legacy and support our heroes to reach the highest levels locally, regionally and globally.
Squash Section

Squash enhances agility, strength, and quick reflexes. It improves coordination, boosts fitness, and sharpens mental focus, making it both challenging and rewarding.

Swimming Section

Swimming builds full-body strength, improves endurance, and enhances flexibility. It promotes cardiovascular health while being low-impact and relaxing, making it a great all-around workout.

Football Section

Football boosts fitness, teamwork, and discipline while improving coordination. It also fosters friendships and teaches valuable life skills, making it both fun and beneficial.
